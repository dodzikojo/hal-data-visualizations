# ACC Role Builder - User Access Request Guide

This guide explains how to use the Role Builder to select roles and submit an access request.

---

## Step 1: Open the Role Builder

1. Navigate to the **ACC Roles Distribution Report** page.
2. Click the **"Role Builder"** tab at the top of the panel.

---

## Step 2: Select a Party (Level 1)

1. You will see a list of available parties (e.g., Lead Designer, Contractor, HAL).
2. **Click on a party** to select it. A checkmark will appear next to your selection.
3. To select multiple parties, toggle **"Multi-select"** on.
4. Use the search box to filter the list if needed.
5. Click **"Next: Select Function →"** to proceed.

---

## Step 3: Select a Function (Level 2)

1. Based on your party selection, a list of available functions will appear (e.g., General, Approver, Checker).
2. **Click on one or more functions** to select them.
3. *Note: "General" is automatically included by default when available.*
4. Click **"Next: Select Discipline →"** to proceed.

---

## Step 4: Select a Discipline (Level 3)

1. Based on your previous selections, available disciplines will appear (e.g., Architecture, Fire, MEP).
2. **Click on one or more disciplines** to select them.
3. *Note: "All" is automatically included by default when available.*
4. Click **"Generate Role List →"** to see your roles.

---

## Step 5: Review Generated Roles

1. You will see a list of all roles that match your selections.
2. Review the list to ensure it includes the roles you need.
3. You can:
   - **Copy individual roles** using the copy button next to each role.
   - **Copy all roles** using the "Copy All" button.
   - **Share the selection** by copying the URL provided.
4. When ready, click **"Next: Request Access →"** to proceed.

---

## Step 6: Submit Access Request

1. In the **"Assignee Emails"** field, enter the email addresses of the people who need these roles.
   - Separate multiple emails with commas or semicolons.
   - Example: `john.doe@example.com, jane.smith@example.com`

2. Click the **"Send Request"** button.

3. Your default email application will open with a pre-filled email containing:
   - Your role selections (Party, Function, Discipline)
   - The list of generated roles
   - The email addresses of the assignees

4. **Review the email** and click **Send** in your email application.

---

## What Happens Next?

- The request will be sent to the **Digital Asset Information Lead, Long-Term Growth**.
- The assignees you listed will be CC'd on the email.
- You will receive confirmation once the roles have been assigned.

---

## Tips

- **Use Multi-select** to choose multiple parties, functions, or disciplines at once.
- **Use the Search** feature to quickly find specific items in long lists.
- **Copy the URL** to share your role configuration with colleagues before submitting.

---

*For questions or assistance, contact the Digital Asset Information Lead.*
